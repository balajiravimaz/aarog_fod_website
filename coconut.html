Shredded Coconut

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Shredded coconut is a key ingredient in many South Indian cuisines and is known to be a nutritious component in any kitchen.

It is mainly used in baking industry and several curries, snacks, chutneys, sweet and various foods. It is used to enhance the flavor and aroma of a dish.

Our impeccable products is completely natural and come in customized stand-up pouches and the shell itself. They are absolutely reusable and give the artistic look customers look up for.

SHELFLIFE

Fresh shredded coconut should be stored in refrigerator or freezer. Our product can be stored and used up to period of 1 month in refrigerator and more than 2 months in the frozen temperatures.

The product needs to be kept away from aberrant environmental conditions.

PACKAGING MATERIAL

Our primary goal is to abolish the use of non biodegradable materials in our products and we use natural packaging materials that can be reused.

We use processed coconut shells to give a aesthetic look as well as giving our customers a sense of using a biodegradable commodity.

  • ✓ Minerals present in coconut are involved in many functions in our body and is high in manganese which plays a major role in bone health.
  • ✓ It enhances the metabolism of carbohydrates, proteins, and cholesterol. It is found to be rich in copper and iron.
  • ✓ Coconut reduces the risk of heart diseases and stabilizes blood sugar levels.
  • ✓ It also contains powerful antioxidants like gallic acid, caffeic acid, salicylic acid and p-coumaric acid.
