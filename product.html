Shredded Coconut

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Shredded coconut is fresh and organic ingredient that will enhance the food’s flavour and aroma. Since it is a daily used ingredient, we have planned to make it as ready to use product with zero chemicals & zero preservatives.

Coconut is almost added with all the food items like curries, chutneys, vegetables frying, sweets and snacks, cookies, garnishing Indian cuisines etc..

It is packed with coconut shell itself to give the authentic look and also with stand-up pouches with zipper. Both the packages can be reusable and can store some cooking ingredients with these packs.

SHELFLIFE

Fresh shredded coconut should be stored in refrigerator or freezer. It can be stored and used upto 1 month in refrigerator and more than 2 months in freezer.

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

We are using Biodegradable Coconut Shell as a packaging material for grated coconut. Coconut shell is comparatively hard and protective than other packaging materials. Coconut shells are also a biodegradable alternative to hard plastics; these shells are several times stronger than plastic pouches.

They can be processed to create reusable bowls and can also be used for growing plants. This packaging material can enhance the real fragrance of our product and can give us authentic look.

COCONUT SHELL PROCESSING

Coconut has been procured based on its taste and size from the farmers.

  • ✓ Remove the husk using coconut husk peeler.
  • ✓ Removed husk has been further processed to produce Coco Pot.
  • ✓ Segregate the coconut as per the size.
  • ✓ Dip the coconut in the water for sometimes. It will be easy to make two equal shells.
  • ✓ Make the surface of the wetted coconut into smoother with sand paper.
  • ✓ Wipe or wash out the dust over the shell.
  • ✓ Break the coconut into two equal pieces. Then wash it with water to ensure the hygiene.
  • ✓ Grate the coconut (white meat).
  • ✓ Remove the sticky brown layer and make the inner surface of the shell smoother.
  • ✓ Finally for hygienic purpose wash the shell with clean water and dry it.
  • ✓ For captivating look, we will finally make the outer surface to glow

  • ✓ So we prefer Coconut shall to be perfect authentic container to store product.

HOW TO FILL AND STORE?

Once the smooth and glowing coconut shell has been prepared, place food graded shrink wrap inside the shell as an inner layer and fill the grated coconut with specific quantity.

Cover the top of the shell with same food graded shrink wrap by providing some space inside the shell and the pack will be more protective. Eventually we will stick the label over the shell.

After the product is developed with protective packaging, we should store the grated fresh coconut with cold storage to maintain its flavour, colour and to increase its shelf life
